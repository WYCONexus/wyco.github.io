Service Overview

  • WYCO Repairs provides reliable property maintenance and repair services for homeowners, landlords, and property managers. Services focus on practical, everyday fixes that keep homes and rental properties safe, functional, and well maintained. From small repairs to preventative maintenance tasks, the goal is straightforward work done correctly and efficiently.

Service Area

  • WYCO Repairs primarily serves the greater Des Moines area and surrounding communities in central Iowa. Service availability may vary depending on scheduling and project scope, but most work is focused within a reasonable driving distance of the Des Moines metro area.
